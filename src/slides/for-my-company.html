Whats in it for my company?

Allowing or promoting contributions can:

  • Help find and recruit top talent
  • Gain influence in the direction of tools you depend on
  • Increase subject matter expertise in developers
  • Reduce in house maintenance

Also, from the 2016 Open Source Jobs Report:

  • 86% of open source pros agree knowing open source has advanced their careers, but... Only 2% say money and perks are the best part of their job
  • 31% most appreciate the interesting projects they work on
  • 18% are most excited about working with cutting edge technologies
  • 17% are motivated by working with a global community
