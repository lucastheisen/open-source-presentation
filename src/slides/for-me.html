Whats in it for me?

Contributing provides an opportunity to:

  • Gain expertise
  • Work on a project that aligns with your interests
  • Interact with a diverse community
  • Give back
  • Build your resume

From the 2016 Open Source Jobs Report:

  • 87% of employers say its hard to find open source talent
  • 59% of hiring managers are seeking additional open source talent
  • 73% of open source pros have been contacted by a recruiter in the last six months
